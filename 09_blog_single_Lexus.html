Toyota Motor Co. Ltd., Toyota-shi, Aichi-ken, Japan On a hot August day in 1983 at the headquarters of Toyota, at a secret meeting of the board of directors, chaired by Chairman of the Board Eiji Toyoda, an important decision was made - to break into the sector of expensive prestigious cars. where Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Jaguar and the "Americans" reigned supreme. To implement the plans, a special secret group F1 was created.

At first, the group's goals were rather modest - to create a new flagship of Toyota, which would rise one step above the Cressida model, which was well known in Japan at that time. But then the researchers of the secret problem realized that if they wanted to break the worldwide image of the Japanese car as an inexpensive, mass product, then they had to enter the foreign market with a new brand that had nothing to do with the Toyota name.
