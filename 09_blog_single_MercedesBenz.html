History of the creation of Mercedes-Benz

April 1, 2020 Admin
Cars, vehicle
7 Comments

Mercedes-Benz is a large German corporation, the name of which is known to every car enthusiast anywhere in the world. Thanks to the successful activities of the concern, as well as the dedication of its leaders, the company managed to break out into the top three leading car manufacturers in the world. And thanks to the availability of cars and the high quality of their assembly, the brand has become popular not only at home, in Germany, but also far beyond its borders. Today, Mercedes-Benz, headquartered in Stuttgart, is a subsidiary of the Deimler-Benz concern. Mercedes-Benz, as a company and trade mark, was founded in 1926.

The history of the brand began its countdown after the death of Gottlieb Daimler in 1900, when Wilhelm Maybach, a friend and associate of Daimler, took over the management of the then unregistered company.
