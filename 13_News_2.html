Mansory tunes the Porsche Cayenne to 700 hp

September 17, 2020 Admin
Cars, vehicle
7 Comments

The top-of-the-line Porsche 718s with 4.0-liter six-cylinder boxer engines are now available with a 7-speed PDK robot with two clutches. The new box is offered for the 718 Boxster and Cayman GTS 4.0, as well as for the top-end 718 Spyder and 718 Cayman GT4 models.

The introduction of a new, faster gearbox has improved the dynamics of sports cars. Compared to similar "three-pedal" modifications, the robotic 420-horsepower 718 Spyder and 718 Cayman GT4 accelerate from 0 to 100 km / h half a second faster in 3.9 seconds, and overcome the 200 km / h mark in 13.4 seconds, 0.4 seconds faster. The acceleration dynamics to 100 km / h of the 718 Cayman GTS 4.0 and 718 Boxster GTS 4.0, with 400 hp engines, also improved by half a second to 4.0 seconds. For the same 0.4 seconds, the acceleration to 200 km / h was also reduced.

The top-of-the-line Porsche 718s with 4.0-liter six-cylinder boxer engines are now available with a 7-speed PDK robot with two clutches. The new box is offered for the 718 Boxster and Cayman GTS 4.0, as well as for the top-end 718 Spyder and 718 Cayman GT4 models.

On the 718 GTS 4.0, the standard Sport Chrono Package adapts the PDK shift pattern to the selected driving mode: Normal, Sport, Sport Plus and Individual. In Sport mode, it enables faster gear changes and earlier downshifts with automatic gear changes, and ensures optimal acceleration through later upshifts. The Sport Plus mode integrates the Launch Control function for an automated start in race mode. The Sport Response button allows the driver to engage the full engine and transmission capabilities for 20 seconds regardless of the current driving mode.

The interiors of the 2021 Porsche 718 are finished with a new Race-Tex material, derived from the material used in racing seats. They replaced the previously used Alcantara. The body palette of the 718 Cayman GT4 and 718 Spyder has been supplemented with a new shade of Python Green. The Porsche 718 Spyder now has 20-inch gold wheels, previously exclusively available on the 718 Cayman GT4. Instead of the base UHP tires, owners will be able to equip the roadster with standard summer tires of similar sizes.
